Our Iterative Development Process

01

Custom website Development

We’ll start with a discovery meeting, understanding what you really want to get out of your new website, as well as properly scope and plan all deliverables. This is where we set up project milestones, identify the resources and skill sets required for the same, discuss the project budget and mitigation strategies and perform an in-depth competitor analysis, before creating the relevant documentation and outlining the project plan of what is to be delivered.

02

Wireframes

Wireframes help you visualize how your website will work before adding more realistic content, fonts, image dimensions or button styles .We need to ensure the user experience is engaging and creative but also intuitive. We create wireframes first, using text and image blocks as well as call to actions to create a prototype you can click through to give you an idea of all the user journeys, followed by a visual design of how your website will look and feel.

03

UI/UX

This is where we get our creative juices flowing. As a web design company in Dubai, All designs are crafted meticulously, making sure they complement your brand and give you an edge over competition while providing your users with an engaging and refreshing experience. After we have reviewed the mockups with you, we will make any changes where necessary. Once approved, we’ll be ready to move onto development.

04

Custom website Development

Behind every world-class website is great code. Our multidisciplinary, full-stack development team will breathe life into your custom website by leveraging a variety of modern, well-supported programming languages and technologies, alongside proven libraries and tools, to make sure the website is slick, assessable, cross-compatible, and mobile-friendly. We fuse great design with fantastic performance, building infrastructures that work flawlessly to create an unbelievable digital experience for the user.

05

Testing

So, we’re almost done! Before the website goes live, we check for any possible glitches and bugs that may have been left undetected.. As an expert web design company in Dubai, we test your website through both manual and automated techniques to make sure any issues are under control and your website serves its intended purpose.

06

Launch & Deployment

Once you are happy with the website and the final QA has been performed, we deploy your website and continually monitor it to flag and resolve any issues. Our experts set up continuous feedback and monitoring procedures to ensure that performance benchmarks are met always.
